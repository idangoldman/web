Having a critical bug in production changes a man. Especially when a user tells you about a bug in the main feature of the application. Can you guess what happened?

Fixing the bug took a few minutes but the embarrassment! Oh, the horror! So, I have decided it’s time to install error tracking software, I have chosen [Sentry](https://sentry.io). Now, I will be notified about the bugs automatically and will fix them sooner. 😇
