## Hi Folks, I’m back on the market! 🥳

Well, that was short and sweet! You know how it goes, sometimes a role and a person just aren't the match they looked like at first, and a few months down the road you both see it clearly.

No hard feelings! I learned, reminded, and validated a lot in the last few months which all comes down to: it's never been about a title, a headcount, or 10x engineering for me. It's always been about architecture, standards, and the tooling that makes a codebase healthy enough for everyone to be better and enjoy their work time.

## So, folks: I’m back on the market! 📢

Frontend architecture, CI/CD pipelines and workflows, platform tooling, codebase health, test coverage, and developer experience are my guilty pleasures. You know the quiet infrastructure that makes a whole team better.
If that's the kind of problem your team is sitting on, let's talk. 👋
