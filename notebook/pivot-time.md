## Hello, how are you doing?

Yes, you read it right! One thing led to another (the wireframe from the previous update) and I have decided to make a product pivot to solve a different problem.

## Why?

While talking with you and other potential target audience. I have realized, you and others already have a pretty solid solution to the problem of creating Coming Soon pages and don’t need another page builder for that.

After a brainstorm session, a new problem arose: “designers, developers, and webmasters, spend a lot of time on moving the envelope from design to code to a content management system to the server. Also, they spend a lot of time on learning technologies for project X on demand from their clients”.

This problem led me to the question of “how to create a tool with design and code abilities to help designers, developers, and webmasters save time”.

## How?

So, I have stopped the development of StaticPage as you know it today and went back to research the problem and question above. I have started interviewing some of you and other professionals in the fields of design and coding.

Stay tuned for next month’s update and conclusions!

**P.S.** If you have any problem with using the current StaticPage let me know and I will help you.

Meanwhile, have fun and enjoy the month! 🤓
Idan.
